Food and Drink

I have listed some of my personal recommendations of restaurants, cafe's and pubs below around Manchester, particularly in and around the city centre area.

Italian Restaurants

San Carlo

An excellent restaurant just off Deansgate. Has a huge selection of wines and fantastic food. Also has other branches around the city such as San Carlo Chichetti and San Carlo Fumo which have more of a tapas style. Other branches of this restaraunt are all over the UK.

San Carlo

Gusto

A faily friendly restaurant situated on Deansgate, and there are other branches in Didsbury and Alderley Edge.

Gusto

Don Giovanni

A fantastic restaurant located in St. Peter's square, and a 3 minute walk from the Bridgwater Hall and across the road from the Central Library.

Don Giovanni

Piccolino's

A posh restaurant with excellent food located near the Town Hall and an extensive menu. Other branches of this restaurant further out in Manchester/Cheshire are in Didsbury, Wilmslow, and Alderley Edge.

Piccolino's

Indian Restaurants

Rajdoot

An authentic indian restaurant right by the Town Hall.

Rajdoot

The Indian Tiffin Room

A family-friendly indian restaurant situated in First Street a 5-minute walk from the Bridgewater Hall.

Indian Tiffin Room

Dishoom

Located Just off Deansgate where the Freemason's Hall on Bridge Street is.

Dishoom

Zouk

A perfect indian restaurant, good for students, based around Manchester. Located just 5-minutes walk from the Palace Theatre and 10-minutes fron the universities.

Zouk

Other Restaurants

Tai Wu

An excelent and authentic chinese restaurant based on Upper Brook Street near to the Manchester universities.

Tai Wu

Bem Brasil

A fantastic brazilian buffet just off Deansgate. A perfect place to go if you are a meat lovers.

Bem Brasil

Australasia

A posh Australian restaurant in Spinningfields.

Australasia

Gaucho

An expensive steak restaurant in central Manchester.

Gaucho

Dimitri's

A fantastic greek restaurant on Deansgate. It has a lovely conservatory, and also a bar too. It can be noisy depending where you are seated in the restuarant and the time of day. Excellent food though!

Dimitri's

Fig and Sparrow

Transport

The transport around Manchester is excellent. There are many good bus routes around Manchester. There is also a tram to take you around the city and across other parts of Manchester on various different routes. There is also a multiple train stations so you travel between places within different parts of Manchester and across the country.
