Attractions and things to do

Shopping

Manchester Arndale

Manchester Arndale is the main shopping centre in central Manchester. It has many shops and also has a market inside it.

Manchester Arndale

Trafford Centre

Trafford Centre is a shopping centre based in South Manchester quite close to the Old Trafford Football stadium. The centre is even bigger than the Arndale and it features a big food court as well as lots of shops. Also has a cinema, bowling and Mini Golf.

Trafford Centre

Quayside, Media City

The Quayside at Media City which used to be called 'The Lowry Outlet Mall' is based in Salford quays. This shopping centre is the smallest of the three.

Quayside

Music

Bridgewater Hall

This famous concert hall a 3-minute walk from the central has a variety of all different sorts of music from all genres including pop artists and various orchestras.

Bridgewater Hall

Stoller Hall

This hall is part of Chetham's School of Music and situated right opposite Manchester Victoria train/tram station. This is a smaller hall than the Bridgewater Hall and also has a wide variety of concerts.

Stoller Hall

AO Arena

Like above this AO Arena, used to be called the MEN Arena, is also very close to Manchester Victoria train/tram station. This arena is a very large hall of over 20k seats and features world-wide pop artists who travel all over the world.

AO Arena

Co-op Live Arena

This newly built hall called the Co-op Live Arena which opened last month in May 2024 is situated right in the Etihad Campus next to a football stadium. Like the AO Arena, lots of pop artists and other musicians all over the world perform here.

Co-op Live Arena

Theatre

Palace Theatre

The Palace Theatre is located just opposite Manchester Oxford Road Train station and is a 15 minute walk from the Manchester University area and a 15 minute walk from Manchester Picadilly Gardens. It is also positioned right next to the Palace Hotel.

Palace Theatre

The Lowry

This Lowry theatre is located in Salford Quays. As well as it being a theatre, the Lowry has all various art exhibitions on in the building too.

The Lowry

Royal Exchange Theatre

The Royal Exchange theatre which is in the centre of town is located just 5 minutes walk from the Mancehster Arndale shopping centre. It is a farily small theatre and the audtitorium is circular shaped and a lot of plays are performed here.

The Royal Exchange Theatre

Museums and Art galleries

Manchester Museum

The Manchester Museum is located in the Manchester University area on Oxford Road and part of the university. It has a lot of exhibitions there to see.

Manchester Museum

Science and Industry Museum

The Museum of Science and Industry is situated in Castlefield, just over Deansgate, and has all sorts of exhibitions to see here on transport and cotton. There is also a steam railway you can go on, there is a flight simulator ride, and also various talks given.

Science and Industry Museum

Imperial War Museum North

The Imperial War Museum North is one of a few groups of these war museums across the country. It is all about the history of the war. It is situated in Salford Quays.

Imperial War Museum North

National Football Museum

This museum is based in the Urbis, just a 3-minute walk from Manchester Victoria station and the Printworks, and also a 5-minute walk from Manchester Arndale. A perfect museum to go to if you are a football lover.

National Football Museum

People's History Museum

This museum is all about the history of Britain, the current and future about Britain, and about democracy. It is located in Spinningfields.

People's History Museum

Manchester Art Gallery

The Manchester Art Gallery is in central Manchester, roughly a 5-minute walk from Picadilly Gardens. Has lots of different exhibitions and portraits in the gallery. Perfect for art lovers.

Manchester Art Gallery

Other fun things to do

Breakout Escape Rooms

A perfect attraction for puzzle lovers! You have locked in a room for 60-minutes in a team and have to solve lots of puzzles and you have to escape within that set time. There are plenty of different rooms to choose between. There is a more than one different location for Breakout escape rooms in Manchester, one located near Manchester Town Hall, and one near Picadilly Gardens.

Breakout Escape Rooms

Chill Factore

This attraction is perfect for skiing and snowboarding lovers. It is an indoor attraction based near to the Trafford Centre.

Chill Factore

Mini Golf and Bowling at the Trafford Centre

Please see info above on Trafford Centre website.

Sport

Old Trafford Football Stadium

A football stadium where Manchester United are based in South Manchester. There is aalso a museum based here at Old Trafford.

Old Trafford Football Stadium

Emirates Old Trafford Cricket Ground

This cricket ground based in Trafford but in a separate location to the Football Stadium.

Emirates Old Trafford Cricket Ground

Etihad Stadium

A football stadium located in East Manchester where Manchester City are based.

Etihad Stadium

Parks

Whitworth Park

A park based near Manchester's famous curry mile in Rusholme, South Manchester, and close to the universities. This park features a gallery too called the Whitworth Gallery.

Whitworth Park Whitworth Art Gallery

Platt Fields Park

This large park based in Fallowfield, South Manchester, where lots of students like to hang out in the summer.

Platt Fields Park

Alexandra Park

This park based in Whalley Range, in South Manchester and has a beautiful lake.

Alexandra Park

Fletcher Moss Park

Based in Didsbury, South Manchester, this park is a perfect walking place to go to.

Fletcher Moss Park and Gardens

Heaton Park

A very large park based a bit further away from the city centre in North Manchester. Easily accessibly by tram.

Heaton Park

Other buildings to see

Central Library

A famous library in the centre of Manchester based in St Peter's Square.

Central Library

Manchester Town Hall

A victorian building based in central manchester and situated right next to where the Central Library.

Manchester Town Hall

Manchester Cathedral

A famous Cathedral based in Manchester which is a few minutes walk from Manchester Victoria station and the Manchester Arndale. There are lots of events, services and cocnerts that take place here.

Manchester Cathedral
