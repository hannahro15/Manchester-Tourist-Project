Attractions and things to do

Shopping

Manchester Arndale

Manchester Arndale is the main shopping centre in central Manchester. It has many shops and also has a market inside it.

Manchester Arndale

Trafford Centre

Trafford Centre is a shopping centre based in South Manchester quite close to the Old Trafford Football stadium. The centre is even bigger than the Arndale and it features a big food court as well as lots of shops. Also has a cinema, bowling and Mini Golf.

Trafford Centre

Quayside, Media City

The Quayside at Media City which used to be called 'The Lowry Outlet Mall' is based in Salford quays. This shopping centre is the smallest of the three.

Quayside

Music

Bridgewater Hall

This famous concert hall a 3-minute walk from the central has a variety of all different sorts of music from all genres including pop artists and various orchestras.

Bridgewater Hall

Stoller Hall

This hall is part of Chetham's School of Music and situated right opposite Manchester Victoria train/tram station. This is a smaller hall than the Bridgewater Hall and also has a wide variety of concerts.

Stoller Hall

AO Arena

Like above this AO Arena, used to be called the MEN Arena, is also very close to Manchester Victoria train/tram station. This arena is a very large hall of over 20k seats and features world-wide pop artists who travel all over the world.

AO Arena

Co-op Live Arena

This newly built hall called the Co-op Live Arena which opened last month in May 2024 is situated right in the Etihad Campus next to a football stadium. Like the AO Arena, lots of pop artists and other musicians all over the world perform here.

Co-op Live Arena

Theatre

Palace Theatre

The Palace Theatre is located just opposite Manchester Oxford Road Train station and is a 15 minute walk from the Manchester University area and a 15 minute walk from Manchester Picadilly Gardens. It is also positioned right next to the Palace Hotel.

Palace Theatre

The Lowry

This Lowry theatre is located in Salford Quays. As well as it being a theatre, the Lowry has all various art exhibitions on in the building too.

The Lowry

Royal Exchange Theatre

The Royal Exchange theatre which is in the centre of town is located just 5 minutes walk from the Mancehster Arndale shopping centre. It is a farily small theatre and the audtitorium is circular shaped and a lot of plays are performed here.

The Royal Exchange Theatre
